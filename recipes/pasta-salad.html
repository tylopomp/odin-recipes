Pasta salad

With this pasta salad, you'll become king of the cookout
It is a well balanced side dish fit for any reveler of barbecues.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of tricolor spiral pasta
  • 1 Bottle of Italian dressing, store bought or homemade
  • Salad seasoning mix
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • 3 Bell peppers, 1 green, 1 yellow, and 1 red
  • 1 Can of black olives
  • 2 cups of feta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook and strain pasta
  2. Add dressing and seasoning mix to bowl and whisk
  3. Wash and cut up vegetables
  4. Combine pasta and vegetables in large serving bowl
  5. Add dressing and feta cheese to large serving bowl, and toss that salad
